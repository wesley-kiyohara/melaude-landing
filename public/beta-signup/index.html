melaude is a digital space where musicians can audition for bands, scout and recruit talent, and collaborate together on the projects that define the world's music scenes.

GLOBAL AUDITIONS

Skip craigslist ads & paper flyers. melaude allows you to share audition videos, recordings, & other content online to try out for bands located anywhere throughout the world.

TALENT SCOUTING

In need of a guitarist, vocalist, or _____ ? Start your own band or project by using melaude's network to find & vet potential band members. Discover, scout, & connect with fellow musicians from your local area & beyond.

MATCH RECOMMENDATIONS

Based on your musical preferences, melaude shows you the most relevant audition opportunities so you don't miss a single chance to try out for the bands & projects that best fit you.
